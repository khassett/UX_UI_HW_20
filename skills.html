MY SKILLS

User Research

User research is a fundamental step in my design process. Getting to know the users allows me to empathize and understand users and their needs.

Here are some of my key user research skills:

  • Create surveys
  • Conduct user interviews
  • Organize key research findings
  • Organize key research findings

Design

Throughout my design process I am always thinking about the user and their needs. I work to reduce pain points and ease accessibility for users, and never stop iterating. This is where I develop style guides and integrate artistic elements into the product. Here are some of my design skills:

  • Create digital art using Procreate
  • Contrast checking
  • Develop color palettes
  • Produce style guides
  • Use grids

User Testing

User testing is vital in receiving feedback to improve design. After developing a low fidelity prototype, I begin my testing. I analyze findings after conducting the usability tests to understand what I need to work on to improve the user experience for the next round of testing. Here are some of my skills I use when testing:

  • Articulate the goals clearly
  • Record testing notes
  • Listen to feedback
  • Learn from the user
