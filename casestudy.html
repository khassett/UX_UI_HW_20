Narika is a non-profit organization founded in 1992 to promote women's empowerment by assisting victims of domestic violence, and trafficking. Narika provides advocacy and support to the women while helping them attain mentally and financial literacy, so they can become self-sufficient.

User Interview Plan

We identified three tasks our users would be performing on the website: Volunteering, Seeking help, and using the navigation bar.

Our user research interview plan focuses on:
How people feel about the current Narika Website
How accessible, usable, and intuitive is the site?

User Insights

We had some consistent feedback from our user interviews about the current site:

  • No clarity on what the site is about or call to action.
  • Both the navigation bar and footer are long and lengthy.
  • Issues with organization and easy access to content.
  • Hero images are unclear and create confusion throughout the site.
  • Allies section placement and carousel speed are awkward.
